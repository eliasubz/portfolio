Job scraper for Iceland

A Scraper works by requesting the HTML file that contains all information of a website. The programmer has to decide whether he wants to store the site or use simple rules to extract certain inormation. I only extract the icelandic level in order to find out whether we could apply to the job listing.

Scientific Approach

Problem

Before I came to Iceland I wanted to find a job. And although this language, that is very hard, is spoken by a total of 315.000 people I decided not to persue icelandic literacy.
Now the problem was that: The biggest job platform in iceland required users to open each job posting to determine the language requirements. In spite of the fact that other filter options,like locations, job types, etc., were available any way of filtering langauges was impossible.

Solution

Automate my job search with a custom-built web scraper. This tool navigates job websites, extracts listings based on the target language competency, and outputs relevant job links.
