Problem Statement

Develop a job scraper that manually navigates job websites, extracts listings based on specific language requirements and returns relevant job links.

My Work

In this project my partener and me tried to filter all jobs that had low to no requirements for icelandic as a language. The website alfred.is is the most comon job site in Iceland. The issue was that the language requirements could not be filtered easily. They would have to be manaully checked individually for each job listing. We created a scraper that goes through all the jobs and analysis the language requirements to make a list of jobs that had langauge requirements that we met.
